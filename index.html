About

With 15+ years in data & analytics, I've built and led data functions at some of the Nordics' most recognised brands, including Pandora, where I established the Retail BI team from scratch. Today, as Director at Inspari, I lead the firm's Retail Data & AI vertical, advising clients on data strategy, platform architecture, and AI-powered analytics solutions that drive measurable business outcomes.

Retail is where I'm most at home. I understand the business pressures: margin, inventory, customer behaviour, omnichannel complexity. And I know how to apply data and AI to decisions that actually move the needle — from demand forecasting and assortment optimisation to real-time operational insights.

My background spans both sides of the table: senior roles inside major retail organisations, and as a consultant and co-founder who built a data company from 0 to 20 people. This gives me an unusually direct understanding of what actually works — not just what looks good in a slide deck.
